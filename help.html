Automatic Weather Stations

A Visualization of Active Antarctic Automatic Weather Stations



To use this data visualization:



Hover or move the mouse over each station to retrieve or access more information on the AWS station as well as the URL to datasets.

Pan and zoom on the map to explore the AWS stations in Antarctica, specifically in the research-intensive Ross Ice Shelf region.

Click the tabs on the left panel to view the visualization for different variables. "About" page includes more information on citing data and the bios of the authors.

Move the slider bar handle to sequence from January 2009 through December 2012 and view the changes of meteorological variables on stations with valid data.
